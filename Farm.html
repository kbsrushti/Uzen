Farm into Kitchen

Johnas 19 March, 2020

In ancient days, people used to eat their produces from the wild. About a few thousand years earlier, people started its agriculture and livestock promotion. This was the inception of farming. And later, people started to commercialize farming by distributing it. After that farming has undergone hefty changes. In this 21st century itself, new technical and hi-tech exposures have tremendously increased to maximize yield and production. Here produces undergoes huge middle handling and transportation. Thus increasing population increasing demand and fewer farmers and more middleman.

Eating locally grown produce is safe and clean, everyone is aware of this. But are they healthier is the question we need to ask ourselves. Searching for pollutant-free, pesticide-free, chemical-free is a sure task for every household individual. Also source of the produces are uncertain. Farm to Kitchen helps people to worry less about the producesâ€™ origin, because Uzen Farms is completely into sustainable farming. Thus, produces are 100% Non-GMO, also completely pesticides and chemical-free. Need not worry about transportation, as only delivery guys are going to handle the fresh produces in delivering it to homes direct from farms, thus 100%.

Uzen Naturals

We at Uzen farms single-mindedly focus on people's health. Synthetic fertilizers, genetically modified organisms, antibiotics, synthetic growth hormones, and artificial preservatives, flavors or colours are strictly not allowed. Farms are under constant supervision.

Growing and delivering produces more hygienically and cleanly. We focus on giving the produces to the locals by delivering the same day harvested produces. Our target is within 5 hrs from the ordered moment, produces will be harvested from the farms and will be delivering it while the green and colorful produce would be still breathing post-delivery also. So you just have to sit back and order online and your fresh produce will be at your doorstep.

Don't judge your produce by it's appearance, but rather judge it by it's harvest.

Uzen Farms Initiatives

Why we don't have enough natural and organic farms in the country? It's because an increase in more the natural farms, farmers' investment to resources, knowledge and efforts are almost doubled. And we here at Uzen, willing to transform and take it back the current agriculture business to the ancient day methods by educating farmers. Uzen Farms is aiming for food self-sufficiency by improving farm techniques, promoting climate-smart agriculture, improving the efficient use of inputs, technology and increasing the knowledge of farmers on Good Agriculture Practices (GAP).

  • Before moving into any supermarket, please don't forget about the farmers markets'.
  • Try smart shopping with Uzen Farms for your produce.
  • Increase your purchase frequency by buying small amount of produce. This will make sure you are eating fresh and healthy produce.
