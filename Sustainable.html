Sustainable Farming and Development - A Road Taken By Uzen

Mark 20 March, 2020

To the improvement of People, To the development of Nation and To safeguard the Earth in way of economy and health, 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDG's) have set in the year 2015 by United Nations. This is a blueprint to achieve a better more sustainable future within 2030. On strategy to improve the health of the nation and to shape global wealth, we young generation side with the United Nation's SDG's to make a better world on the way of Sustainable Farming.

Uzen Farms

There are more talks that we heard outside the world to input the knowledge and techniques for Sustainable Farming. To do the way, Uzen Farms encompasses a smart way of sustainable farming practice to produce healthy produce with Hi-Tech farming viz. Vertical Farming, Hydroponics and Aquaponics, which also increase the knowledge of Farmer and fills the need for Sustainable Development Farming (SDF).

How do Uzen Farms help in Sustainable Farming?

Avoiding Wastage of Water

Uzen Farms completely encompasses cultivation with Hi-Tech Vertical Farming techniques like Hydroponics. But everyone has the question of feeling that this technique consumes more water. That's untrue! Well, Hydroponics method (The process of growing plants without soil, by supplying water with natural nutrients to avoid it from growing in dirt and wasting excess water) and Aquaponics method (The process of growing plants in water by supplying nutrients by the help of aquaculture). Moreover, these above two methods save water from more than 10 times the traditional field cropping method.

Biodegradable farming

When we take an oath for Sustainable Farming, productions done in Uzen Farms are completely green. We don't use or need bio-degradable materials in the cultivation process apart from the items like germination tray, seedling cups for the initial nurturing of the plants. They are also reused and preserved properly for the next best use. The nutrients we use are highly natural and passed in the way of scheduled level with a day by day care.

A clean green world with healthy foods

Nowadays foods from normal cropping methods are injected with pests to get rid of insects on the soil. How about food that grows with the same health without any traces of pests? Uzen Farms grows such healthy food only in water rather than in dirt and spending excess water on the ground. Our gradual feeding of organic nutrients and caring grows healthy plants to the world with a hardihood.

Great job opportunities for youth

We provide opportunities for youngsters who are interested in and love farming. Never need an education, we give training and employment in our farming. We also encourage new inventions for the growth of organic hydro farming. So, it's a never miss chance for every youngster.
