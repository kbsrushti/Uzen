Our Story

We have heard of Sustainable Farming, but how about its effectiveness in an urban eco space?
Is it possible? We thought… We said… Let’s do it!

Why farming in Urban area?

Our focus is to deliver clean and fresh produce with their nutrient values intact. So why urban farming? we found out freshness and health of the produces which is handled and transported from hundreds of KMs deplete over time.

Why Banglore?

Banglore has always been a place that is known for assorted sectors like Healthcare, Education, Manufacturing Textiles and IT. We conducted a survey in the city about their mindset about the food they eat. Banglorians are also moving their thoughts towards health and quality - Especially Food. And in recent days, a huge increase in demand for locally grown and organic produce. Said this, even our entire team are Banglorians

How is the amalgamation of farming with technology?

We engineers turned farmers know technology has played a huge part in farming already, but we still wanted it to be sustainable in a city environment. Hi-Tech farming is our main business, and we do vertical farming techniques viz. Hydroponics, Aquaponics, Organic Vertical Farming.

Do we bring it home?

Bringing it home is our primary motto. We have taken up a promising task of delivering fresh produce within 5 hrs from the time of harvest. So, we cultivate and harvest produce locally. People just have to sit back and order directly from our website.

What's in for the community?

We cut out the middleman, thus we get the money directly to our farmers. Our Uzen Hunger Campaign - Vithaikal, which focuses on reducing hunger of the city by various activities by our farmers and team.

Why the name UZEN?

We have come across this question often :D Frankly there is no meaning for this word. Probably this is another figment of word stuck in our head.
